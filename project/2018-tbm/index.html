Underground Disc-cutter Replacement Robot for Tunnel Boring Machine

2020-06-01
The TBM cutter head

Specialized robot that helps release humans from dangerous TBM maintenance works underground.

Tunnel Boring Machine (TBM) is a kind of specialized engineering machine for excavating underground tunnels. The modern TBMs are technology-intensive systems that can perform various comprehensive tasks such as tunnel boring, excavated material removal and tunnel structure supporting at the same time, which greatly improves the overall project efficiency, quality and safety. The cutting head part of a shield TBM is specialized for underground hard rock material tunneling (see the featured figure).

The disc cutters, arranged at the forefront cutting head part of the TBM, are the main cutting tools for cutting hard rock materials. The disc cutters are pressed onto the cutting surface with enough force so that the rock structure will crash as a result. As the cutting head rotates, the disc cutters will rotate round its own shaft and move along circle path on the cutting surface at the same time to form an even full-surface cutting result. The disc cutters will endure large working load to meet the rock crushing result, and they work in a complex extreme situation underground, with high pressure, high humidity and high temperature, so that the disc cutter worn-out and faulty is quite often during the boring process, which may affect the work efficiency and quality or even endanger the TBM system. Therefore, the regular replacement of the worn/damaged disc cutter in time is crucial for the normal running of the TBM machine, maintaining high operation productivity and keeping the system safety.

This project aims develop unmanned robot that can help release humans from dangerous TBM maintenance works underground.
